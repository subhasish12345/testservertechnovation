Idea presentation

Idea Presentation is an event where participants pitch their innovative ideas or projects to an audience, typically including judges or potential investors. The goal is to effectively communicate the value, feasibility, and impact of their idea.

Eligibility

1.Participants must register before the deadline.

2.Individual or team (maximum 4) can participate.

3.Participants must be original creators of the idea.

Presentation Format:

1.Each presentation should focus on a single idea. 2.Presentation length: 5-7 minutes per presentation.

3.Visual aids: PowerPoint slides. 4.Language of the presentation: English.

Judging Criteria

1.Originality and Creativity: The idea should be innovative and demonstrate creative thinking. 2.Feasibility: Judges will assess whether the idea is practical and implementable. 3.Impact: The idea should have a positive social, environmental, or business impact. 4.Presentation Skills:Clear communication, engagement with the audience, and confidence during the presentation. 5.Supporting Evidence: Ideas should be backed by data, research, or real-world examples, where applicable.
