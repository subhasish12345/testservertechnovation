Group Discussion

Group Discussion is It is a form of structured discussion conducted among participants on a specific topic to evaluate their communication skills, knowledge, leadership abilities, and teamwork. It is widely used in recruitment processes and entrance exams.

Rules:

A team comprising of 5 members each will take part in GD on a topic given for 10 min.Three teams will qualify for 2nd round. Each team will compete on a subject given to them for a duration of 5 min for the final 2nd round. Only one team will be chosen as winner in the 2nd round.

Topic:

Topic: • Abstract Topics: These are broad and open-ended, like “Innovation is the key to success” or “Red vs. Blue.” • Current Affairs: Based on recent events like “Impact of AI on Jobs” or “Sustainable Development in Business.” • Case-Based Topics: Present a hypothetical situation or business problem to solve. • Controversial Topics: Topics that spark debate, such as “Globalization is a threat to local businesses.”

Evaluation Criteria:

• Content Knowledge: How well participants understand the topic and support their points with facts or examples. • Clarity & Communication: Clear articulation of thoughts with proper structure. • Listening Skills: Acknowledging and building on other participants' viewpoints. • Teamwork: Respecting others' opinions, encouraging quieter participants, and maintaining the group’s focus. • Leadership & Initiative: Taking charge of the discussion without dominating it.
