Human-AI collaboration challenge

Human-AI collaboration challenge is A fun and tech-centric event idea related to AI that combines image generation, storytelling, and video creation

Rules:

The event would involve teams or individuals using AI tools to create unique narratives, images, and short videos. Participants will be given a set of prompts or themes (e.g., "Exploring the Future," "Parallel Universes," or "Mythical Creatures" etc...), and they must utilize AI platforms to generate content within a limited timeframe.

Event Structure: First from every team a single member will come and pick up a chit , with a theme written on it , after analysis about their theme , the event will proceed further .

1. *Round 1: AI-Generated Story* Participants start by using AI writing tools to generate an original short story or narrative based on the provided theme. They can collaborate with AI to develop creative, unexpected twists.

2. *Round 2: AI Art* Using AI image generators (like DALL-E or similar tools), participants will create a series of images that visually represent key scenes, characters, or settings from their stories.

3. *Round 3: AI Video Creation* In this round, the participants will use AI video tools to create a short video or animation based on their story and visuals. The video should tie everything together, using music, visuals, and minimal text to present a narrative.
