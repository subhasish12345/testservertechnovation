Business Quiz

Business Quiz is an intellectual competition that tests participants' knowledge of various business-related topics. It typically involves multiple rounds where individuals answer questions about the business world.

Rules:

Individual : Individual students are required to participate in this event.

Rounds: The quiz typically includes a preliminary round and a final round.

Format: Rounds vary, including MCQs and rapid fire.

Scoring: Correct answers earn points, while incorrect answers may incur negative points.

Time Limits: Teams have a set amount of time to answer each question. Tie Breakers: Tie-breakers may be used to determine the winner in case of a tie.

Code of Conduct: Participants must adhere to rules, avoid cheating, and maintain proper behavior. Judging: The quizmaster's decision is final on all matters.

Prizes: Prizes are awarded to the top-performing student. Registration: Teams must register by the deadline set by the organizers.
