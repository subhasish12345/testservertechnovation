Star Investor

Description: Students will invest in the Indian stock market in a real-time basis via virtual money. Everyone starts with equal capital. The person who makes the most money wins.

Game Overview:

• Objective: Maximize your virtual portfolio's value by the end of the event.

• Participants: Open to all students, regardless of prior trading experience.

• Duration: The event spans 3 rounds. ( The event registration needs to be over, 7 days prior to the event, so that demo workshop and account login can be completed well before the event starts )

Game Rules:

1. Starting Capital: Each participant receives ₹10 lakhs of virtual money in a dummy account. 2. Investment Options: Participants can invest in equities, futures, and options. 3. No Transaction Limits: You can make an unlimited number of trades during the event. 4. Winning Criteria: The top 3 participants with the highest portfolio value at the end of Day 3 will be declared winners and titled "Star Investors."

Additional Information:

• Workshop: A brief workshop will be conducted for participants without prior trading experience to help them understand the basics of trading. The rules can be modified as per the overall requirements of the event.
