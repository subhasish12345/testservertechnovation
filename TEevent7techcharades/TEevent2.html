Tech Charades

Tech Charades is a fun and interactive event where participants act out technology-related terms, concepts, or professions without speaking, while others guess what they are representing. This game combines creativity with tech knowledge and is great for team-building or ice-breaking activities.

Rules:

1. Team Formation: Participants are divided into teams of 3-5 members. 2. Turn-Based Acting: Teams take turns with one member acting out a tech-related term or concept while the rest of the team guesses.

3. Time Limit: Each turn has a set time limit (e.g., 1-2 minutes) for acting and guessing. 4. No Speaking: The person acting cannot speak or use any words; they can only use gestures, body language, and miming.

5. Clues and Hints: No verbal or written clues are allowed, but participants can use props if the event rules allow. 6. Scoring: Points are awarded based on how quickly the team guesses the term correctly. Each correct guess within the time limit earns points.

7. Term Selection: Terms are typically drawn from a hat or chosen from a pre-prepared list. Terms should be related to technology, such as programming languages, tech gadgets, famous tech figures, or concepts. 8. Winning Criteria: The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.
